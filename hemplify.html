Welcome to Hempify!

Hempify is a web app that helps you grow hemp and fight climate change. Here you can find everything you need to start your hemp journey, from planting to harvesting, from buying to selling, from learning to earning. Join the hemp revolution today!

Grow

Learn how to grow hemp in your backyard or balcony. Follow our step-by-step guide and track your progress. See how much carbon dioxide you can save by growing hemp.

Shop

Explore our hemp marketplace and discover a variety of hemp products. Buy and sell hemp products, such as food, clothing, cosmetics, and building materials. Support local hemp growers and businesses.

Connect

Join our hemp community and connect with other hemp enthusiasts. Share your hemp stories and photos, chat with other hemp users, and participate in hemp events and challenges.

Earn

Earn hemp points and badges for growing, buying, and selling hemp. Redeem your hemp points and badges for hemp prizes, such as discounts, coupons, and freebies.
